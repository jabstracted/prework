Bio

Electronic music duo from Toronto, Canada.
Made up of Dylan Mamid – who goes by the name 'DC',
and Zachary Rapp-Rovan – who goes by the name 'Hooks.'

They were introduced to each other by mutual friends in
2004 and have since started their own record label called
DeadBeats in 2016.
